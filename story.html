First Time On Court Together

Lisa and Paul met first on the courts of Fort Washington Park located on the Upper Westside of Manhattan. Lisa was perfecting her overheads with her 8ft tall Ecuadorian Instructor. He called Paul, in his best Roger Federer impression, over to hit together with Lisa. That would be the beginning of their time together on court.


First Date

After spending months hanging out on the courts Paul asked Lisa out to dinner and a movie. Lisa suggested watching Men in Black 3. This was a solid choice and would be one of many excellent movies they would see together.


Making It "Official"

On July 1st they made it official to start going out.


The Proposal

October of 2016 Lisa got eye surgery so she couldn't see. This was when Paul enacted his plan to get the ring he would propose with. After months of trying to find the right time, Paul took Lisa to see La La Land and proposed afterward.




The Next Chapter

Lisa and Paul are preparing for the next chapter in their lives together and welcome all friends and family to be a part of it.
